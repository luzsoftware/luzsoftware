Process

How an idea becomes visible.

Step 01
Spark.
We start inside the idea — not the brief. A week of listening, research, and sketches to surface the one insight everything else will orbit.
Step 02
Prototype.
Working code within two weeks. Not a slide deck. Real software, real models, real pixels — something you can break, test, and fall in love with.
Step 03
Refine.
The hundred invisible decisions. Type, tolerances, motion curves, model evals, copy rhythm — the finish that separates product from prototype.
Step 04
Launch & live with it.
Ship, measure, iterate. We stay on for the first quarter of real users, because the launch is not the end of design — it's the beginning.
